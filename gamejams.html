Game Jam Timeline

Here's a list of (almost) every game jam I've ever done! Some are unfinished, crazy, ridiculous, or just plain terrible, but that's the fun of it! I wanted to catalog all of these jams to show just how far I've come.
There's no way to improve unless you keep creating!

Scale Escape

August 2018
Theme: "Running Out of Space" - Ludum Dare 42 
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: Solo

A size-based Escape Room game. Use your size manipulation powers to push back the walls of a room enclosing around you.

Chain Reaction

May 2018
Theme: "Chain Reaction"
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: 5

A twin-stick bullet hell that's tied to the music. When you kill an enemy, it explodes on the next beat in a barage of bullets. Those bullets will also kill enemies, causing a massive chain reaction! If you fire on beat, your bullets will pierce enemies and increase your combo.

The More You Snow

December 2017
Theme: "The more you have, the worse it is"-LD40
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: Solo

A katamari-style game about rolling a snowball down a hill. Rolling over patches of snow will cause the snowball grow, which in turn makes it harder to control. Reach the end as fast as you can!

Aeromancer

July 2017
Theme: "Running out of power" - LD39
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: 2

In this 2D platformer, you play as a robot with an air-cannon for an arm. Firing the cannon will kill enemies and launch you in the opposite direction at the same time. Reach the end as fast as you can without running out of battery power.
