Game Jam Timeline

Here's a list of every game jam I've ever done! Some are unfinished, crazy, ridiculous, or just plain terrible, but that's the fun of it! I wanted to catalog all of these jams to show just how far I've come. There's no way to improve unless you keep creating!

Open Connection

January 2018
Theme: "Transmission" - GGJ2018
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: 2 

Players take on the role of a hacker trying to match-3 their way to undo the evil machinations of the world's premier megacorp alongside a few of their friends.

The More You Snow

December 2017
Theme: "The more you have, the worse it is"-LD40
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: Solo

A katamari-style game about rolling a snowball down a hill. Rolling over patches of snow will cause the snowball grow, which in turn makes it harder to control. Reach the end as fast as you can!

Aeromancer

July 2017
Theme: "Running out of power" - LD39
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: 2

In this 2D platformer, you play as a robot with an air-cannon for an arm. Firing the cannon will kill enemies and launch you in the opposite direction at the same time. Reach the end as fast as you can without running out of battery power.

A Knightly Run

January 2017
Theme: "You only get one"
Time: 48 Hours
Group Size: 5

A 2-player platforming racing game. Each knight only has one power. Reach the end of the procedurally generated level before your opponent!
